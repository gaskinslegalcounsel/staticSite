About

A career built in the courtroom,
now dedicated to resolution

With four decades as a trial lawyer, Steve Gaskins brings a rare depth of litigation experience to every mediation and arbitration. Having spent a career preparing cases for trial, examining witnesses, and arguing before judges and juries, Steve Gaskins understands what drives parties toward settlement and what it takes to reach a fair outcome.

That courtroom perspective is now focused entirely on helping parties resolve their disputes efficiently, fairly, and without the cost and uncertainty of trial. Whether through mediation or arbitration, clients benefit from a neutral who has seen virtually every type of civil dispute from the inside.

Services

Neutral services tailored to

01

Mediation

A collaborative, confidential process where parties work together with a skilled neutral to craft their own resolution. Effective mediation saves time, reduces costs, and preserves relationships that litigation often destroys.

02

Arbitration

A streamlined alternative to trial where an experienced neutral hears evidence and renders a binding or non-binding decision. Arbitration offers the structure of a courtroom with greater efficiency and flexibility.

03

FINRA Arbitration

Specialized arbitration for securities and investment disputes through the Financial Industry Regulatory Authority. As a qualified FINRA arbitrator, Steve Gaskins brings focused expertise to broker-dealer, investor, and industry disputes.
